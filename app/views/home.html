Wel Come to Date Time Picker

I have developed this component for learning purpose. its based on angular material and momentJs. I have tried to follow material guildlines, however there are some area is left open in material guidlines.

What to do if date and time picker is required? How to display date and time? How to transite from date selection to time selection?

Goal for Date picker

  • Should be design as per Material design guide.
  • Should support Min Max Date.
  • Should provide option to select time with date
  • Should able to change year
  • Should support material theme.
  • Should be supported mobile.
